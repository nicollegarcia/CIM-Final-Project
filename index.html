Biography

Nicolle Garcia was born in Managua, Nicaragua and raised in Miami, FL. Her musical style is a fusion of latin, jazz, and folk largely due to her latin surroundings growing up, and jazz performance education at the Frost School of Music. Nicolle has studied with Grammy-Award winning artist, John Daversa, and has performed for Jazz Legend, Sheila Jordan. As a multi-faceted artist, she is also a photographer and model. When Nicolle isn't performing, modeling, or taking photos, she teaches voice lessons and directs an ensemble at Miami Lighthouse for the Blind.
